Do all birds store food for the winter?

Spring is comming already!

Birds - bullfinch - Chickadees, nuthatches, some woodpeckers, jays, and crows store, or “cache,” food. Many other feeder birds—doves, sparrows, blackbirds, finches, etc.—do not store food at all. Those that do store food may hide hundreds, or even thousands, of seeds every year.
